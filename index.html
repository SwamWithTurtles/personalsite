Coder

I "do" software. I've architected/developed applications and led teams to do the same. I've worked on web applications, back-end microservices and multitenant platforms in the past. Though I'm happy to dive into anything new, I'm most familiar with Java, JavaScript and data technologies (SQL, NoSQL, Kafka etc.).

My recent experience includes Kaluza (setting up and leading the data strategy and data platform teams), CitiGroup (building out a 'Kafka as a Service' platform); and with RBS's innovation lab, building out award-winning challenger bank 'Mettle. I've also worked for multiple consultancies that have given me insight into a wide range of different projects. I've run project reviews, project rescue engagements and training courses, as well as leading the end-to-end delivery of software projects.

You can often find me at conferences discussing all I've found out - there is a list of upcoming talks as well as videos of my previous talks.
